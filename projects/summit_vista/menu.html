Summit Vista

Our menu section is a culinary journey, offering a diverse range of buffet and pre-selected menus. Whether you are in the mood for the robust flavors of Mexican cuisine, the refined simplicity of Continental dishes, or the rich aromas of Italian fare, we have something to tantalize every palate. Choose from our carefully crafted breakfast, lunch and dinner selections to complement your special event, ensuring a memorable feast for you and your guests.

Pre-selected menus

Continental Breakfast

Our Continental menu begins with a crisp Caesar salad, followed by entree choices of herb-crusted salmon or filet mignon. Dessert features a decadent chocolate mousse, paired with a classic Chardonnay or a smooth espresso martini. Its a timeless dining experience that blends tradition with elegance.

Lunch Menu

The lunch selection offers a garden-fresh Caprese salad, with mains of grilled chicken pesto or seared tuna steak. Enjoy a lemon tart for dessert, alongside a light Sauvignon Blanc or a refreshing citrus martini. It is a perfect midday indulgence that is both satisfying and sophisticated.

Dinner Menu

Dinner is a grand affair, starting with a luxurious lobster bisque, followed by braised lamb shank or pan-seared scallops. End on a sweet note with berry cheesecake, complemented by a bold Cabernet Sauvignon or a rich chocolate martini. It is an exquisite blend of flavors that makes for an unforgettable evening.

Buffet menus

Breakfast Menu

Awaken your senses with our breakfast buffet, a morning medley of warm, freshly-baked croissants, vibrant fruit assortments, and a live omelet station. It is the perfect start to any day, offering both healthful choices and indulgent treats for any kind of early event.

Italian Menu

Our Italian buffet is a celebration of flavors, a feast of classic Caesar salad, featuring hand-tossed pizzas, al dente pasta tossed in rich sauces, and a selection of fine wines. It is a taste of Italy that is both authentic and memorable, perfect for any occasion.

Mexican Menu

Our Mexican buffet is a vibrant celebration of flavors, starting with a hearty tortilla soup, leading into a fiesta of taste with spicy street tacos, carne asada, and chicken mole. For dessert, enjoy the sweet finish of churros. With options to sip on sangria or a margarita, it's aninteractive and authentic dining experience that's as lively as the culture it represents.
