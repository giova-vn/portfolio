Hi! My name is Angel

Playing with illumination and shadows, I love to capture the beauty of situations and environments. There can be as much detail in a photo as in a movie. Capturing nature and structures can have a very deep message and story. Capturing emotions and essences in special events is the opportunity to freeze a moment in time in order to relive emotions.

My idea is to be able to capture images that resonate with you. Images that transport you in time and memory. The vastness of a landscape, the elegance of a building, the meaning of a smile or the reason for a meeting. Photography has an infinite number of possibilities. Whatever the situation, I am committed to help you and make your moments timeless.
